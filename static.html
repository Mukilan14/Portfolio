Smart Attendance Management System

smart attendance management system designed to automate the process of recording and monitoring attendance for students or employees. The system allows users to mark attendance digitally, apply for leave or on-duty requests, and view their attendance records. Administrators can manage users, approve or reject requests, track attendance statistics, and generate reports. By replacing manual attendance methods, AttendX improves accuracy, reduces paperwork, prevents errors, and provides real-time attendance monitoring through a user-friendly interface..
